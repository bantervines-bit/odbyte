Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2025

The Simple Version

We respect your privacy. We collect only what we need to run the service. We don't sell your data. Your private prompts stay private. That's it.

What We Collect

  • Account Info: Your name, email, and password (encrypted).
  • Prompts: The content you save (public or private).
  • Usage Data: Basic analytics to improve the app.
  • Payment Info: Processed securely through Razorpay. We don't store card details.

How We Use It

  • To run your account and save your prompts.
  • To show public prompts in the Explore section.
  • To process Premium upgrades.
  • To send important updates (we won't spam you).

Your Rights

You can:

  • Access all your data anytime.
  • Delete your prompts whenever you want.
  • Delete your entire account (email us).
  • Export your data (coming soon).

Security

We use industry-standard encryption and security practices. Your password is hashed. Private prompts are only visible to you. We take security seriously.

Cookies

We use essential cookies to keep you logged in and make the site work. That's it. No tracking cookies or third-party nonsense.

Questions?

Email us at privacy@odbyte.com. We'll get back to you within 48 hours.
