Frequently Asked Questions

Can I upgrade or downgrade anytime?

Yes! You can upgrade to Diamond anytime. If you choose monthly billing, you can cancel anytime. Lifetime users keep access forever.

What happens to my prompts if I downgrade?

Your existing prompts are safe. You just won't be able to create new ones beyond the free limit until next month.

How do creator credits work?

When someone copies your premium pack for the first time, you get 1 prompt saving credit. This means you can save 1 extra prompt that month. Only unique users count.

What's the difference between monthly and lifetime?

Monthly is $5/month, billed monthly. Lifetime is a one-time $49 payment and you get Diamond access forever. Lifetime saves you money if you use it for 10+ months.
